About Us

Excellence in Handling Royal Airport Services (Pvt.) Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 & OHSAS 18001:2007 Certified Ground Handling Company of Pakistan, which right from its inception in year 2006 embarked upon the process to introduce best “Ground Handling Practices” consistent with the International Standards. The tenets of best practices in passenger handling, cargo handling, ramp handling, baggage handling, equipment, occupational safety, security, health standards and human resources are our hall mark. A humble start from a single foreign International Airline handling, today, we proudly handle International flights from six International Airports of Pakistan – Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, Multan and Faisalabad. We are providing Ground Handling Services to almost 70% of foreign Airlines operating to and from Pakistan. It speaks of the confidence reposed in our company and the standards maintained by it as envisioned in our slogan – “Excellence in handling”.
